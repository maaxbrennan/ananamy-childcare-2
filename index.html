About me

Nanny service
Great for parents working long hours! Based in your own home where your children feel most comfortable, I can structure our day around your routine. The children will enjoy plenty of activities indoors and outdoors!
As addition to this I can provide basic cleaning and nutritious home cooked meals for the whole family to enjoy - a favourite with the existing customers who love to return home to a clean home and dinner on the table!

Holiday Care
A great option for working parents with older children, this service gives you the opportunity to maintain your normal working hours through the school holidays knowing your children are experiencing lots of indoor and outdoor fun!
Again this can be offered within your own home, as with the nanny service I can also additionally prepare meals involving the children as much as possible!

Evening Babysitting
This can be arranged on a regular basic or ADHOC.
To be able to accommodate more families I have expanded our team! Each team member has been carefully selected and DBS checked. You can request to view their profiles at any time.

All of our day care involves beach walks, park trips, Forest adventures , swimming and much more.
